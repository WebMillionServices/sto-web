What is StoRegister?

StoRegister is a comprehensive platform specially crafted for Self-Storage Operators to run multiple Storage types under one platform. It comes with a pool of features compatible for management of small, medium, or large enterprises. The inbuilt automation facilitates the operators with the most they need – A strong online presence with Booking & reservation system, payment solution, access control system and automatic synchronisation with other 3rd party tools.
