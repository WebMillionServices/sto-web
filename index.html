What is StoRegister?

StoRegister is a comprehensive platform specially crafted for Self Storage Operators to run Multiple Storage Types under one platform. It comes with a pool of features compatible for management of small, medium, or large enterprises. The Inbuilt Automation facilitates the operators with the best of services – A strong online presence with Booking and Reservation System, Payment Solution, Access Control System and Automatic Synchronization with other 3rd Party Tools.
